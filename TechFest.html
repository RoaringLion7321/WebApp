About Tech Fest

Tech Fest 2026 is the biggest college technology event of the year! Join developers, designers, and innovators for workshops, competitions, networking, and exciting keynote sessions.

The event brings together students, faculty, industry professionals, and tech enthusiasts for a full day of innovation, collaboration, and learning. Participants will engage in hands-on workshops, coding competitions, panel discussions, and networking sessions with leading experts from top tech companies.
